## Core Feature Section

### Section Header
AI-Generated Meditation Music

### Body Copy
Powered by Suno AI, Still transforms your words into original meditation tracks. No two creations are the same.

### How It Works

**1. Choose Your Style**
Select from seven handcrafted presets — Ambient, Nature, Piano, Tibetan, Binaural, Lo-fi, Classical — or define your own custom style.

**2. Describe the Mood**
Tell Still what you're feeling. "A quiet evening by a mountain lake." "Deep breathing with resonant tones." "Floating through warm, golden light." Your words become your music.

**3. Generate and Meditate**
In about a minute, you'll have a unique, high-quality meditation track ready to use. Save it to your library and return to it whenever you need.

## FAQ Section

### Section Header
Questions

**How does the AI music generation work?**
You describe the mood, imagery, or feeling you want. Choose a style (Ambient, Piano, Nature, etc.) and whether you want instrumental or vocals. Still uses Suno AI to generate a completely original meditation track based on your input.

**How long does it take to generate music?**
Typically 1-2 minutes. You'll see a progress indicator while your track is being created.

**Is Still free?**
Yes, Still is free during the public beta. We'll introduce optional premium features in the future, but core functionality will remain accessible.

**Can I use my generated music in meditation sessions?**
Absolutely. Save any track to your library and use it as the soundtrack for timed meditation sessions.

**What devices are supported?**
Still is available for iPhone running iOS 16 or later. iPad support is coming soon.

**Do I need an account?**
No. You can use Still without signing in. Create an account with Apple to sync your progress and library across devices.
